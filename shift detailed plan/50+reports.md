Product Requirements Document: Reports Module
Fuel Station Management System

1. Executive Summary
   The Reports Module serves as the analytical backbone of a multi-business fuel station management system, encompassing four distinct business verticals: Fuel Station, CNG, Lube, and Mart. This module provides 200+ comprehensive reports designed to track every operational, financial, and compliance aspect of the business, enabling data-driven decision-making and complete business visibility.

2. Business Context
   2.1 Purpose
   Enable stakeholders to monitor, analyze, and optimize operations across all four business verticals through granular reporting capabilities that track every transaction, inventory movement, employee activity, and customer interaction.
   2.2 Success Metrics

100% data accuracy across all reports
Report generation time < 5 seconds for daily reports, < 30 seconds for annual reports
95% user satisfaction with report relevance and usability
Zero compliance violations due to reporting gaps

9. Technical Requirements
   9.1 Report Generation Engine

Technology Stack: Compatible with modern BI tools (Tableau, Power BI, Metabase) or custom-built reporting module
Database: Relational database with optimized indexing for fast query performance
Processing: Batch processing for historical reports, real-time for operational dashboards
Scalability: Handle concurrent report generation across multiple users

9.2 User Access & Permissions

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):

Station Manager: All reports for their location
Regional Manager: Consolidated reports across locations
Business Head: Business-specific reports
Finance Team: Financial and accounting reports
Compliance Officer: Compliance and regulatory reports
Auditor: Read-only access to audit trails

Data Security: Encryption at rest and in transit
Audit Logging: Track who accessed which report and when

9.3 Report Delivery Mechanisms

On-Demand: User-initiated report generation through web/mobile interface
Scheduled: Automated report generation and email delivery (daily, weekly, monthly)
Dashboard: Real-time widgets and KPI dashboards
Mobile Alerts: Critical threshold breaches sent via SMS/push notification
API Access: RESTful APIs for third-party integration

9.4 Export & Integration

Export Formats: PDF (for presentation), Excel (for analysis), CSV (for data manipulation), JSON (for system integration)
Integration: APIs to push data to accounting software (Tally, SAP), government portals, bank systems
Archival: Automated report archiving with configurable retention policies

9.5 Performance Benchmarks

Simple reports (<1000 rows): < 3 seconds
Complex reports (>10,000 rows): < 15 seconds
Dashboard refresh: < 2 seconds
Concurrent users: Minimum 50 simultaneous report generations
99.9% uptime SLA

9.6 Data Visualization

Charts: Line, bar, pie, area, combo charts
Tables: Sortable, filterable, with subtotals and grand totals
Heatmaps: For time-of-day patterns, geographic distribution
Trend Lines: For forecasting and pattern recognition
Drill-Down: Click through summary to detail level
