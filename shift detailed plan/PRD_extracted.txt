  SECTION 01  |  Executive Summary
  Problem Statement & System Vision
1.1  The Problem ΓÇö Confirmed Financial Haemorrhage
  The organisation operates 3 fuel stations and has suffered untraced financial losses exceeding PKR 20,00,000 (Twenty Lakh Rupees) with no ability to identify source, date, station, or responsible party.
Root Cause Analysis reveals total absence of transaction granularity:
No record of which station lost money
No link between price changes and stock on hand at time of change
No way to trace which salesman handled which cash during which shift
No reconciliation between digital payments received and bank deposits
No batch-level tracking of fuel received at what purchase rate
No supplier payment tracking matched to deliveries
No shift-level cash accountability for any individual employee
1.2  System Vision
  FuelTrack 360┬░ is a permanent, forensic-grade, legally-admissible record of every litre received, every litre sold, every rupee collected, every rupee paid ΓÇö across all 3 stations ΓÇö from Day 1 to Year 2080. The system is designed so that any loss, any discrepancy, or any staff misbehaviour surfaces within 24 hours, not 20 months later.
The system achieves this through 5 unbreakable guarantees:
Complete Traceability: Any single sale can be traced forward to the bank and backward to the supplier batch
Shift-Level Accountability: Every rupee and litre is owned by a specific salesman in a specific shift
Rate-Locked History: All historical transactions are permanently frozen at the price active at exact time of sale
Immutable Records: No transaction can be deleted; all corrections create visible audit trail entries
Perpetual Readability: Data from 2026 will be perfectly readable and queryable in the year 2080

Domain 1: Station Foundation
  A    Station Master Configuration   [CORE]

Nozzle registry: nozzle ID, fuel type, pump number, meter reading baseline, installation date, calibration certificate
Tank registry: tank ID, fuel type, capacity in litres, safe fill level, current stock, dip-stick calibration table (offset per cm)

Station-level settings: allowed payment methods, credit customer access, expense approval chain
  B    Price Rate Engine   [CRITICAL]
Per-fuel price table: Petrol, HSD, HOBC, Kerosene, LPG ΓÇö each independently configurable
Effective date and time for every price change stored to the second ΓÇö no rounding to midnight
Price change workflow: draft ΓåÆ manager review ΓåÆ owner approval ΓåÆ scheduled activation
Pre-rate and post-rate clearly separated; all historical transactions permanently frozen at the rate active at exact transaction timestamp
Pending price change queue: set tomorrow's rate today, system auto-activates at configured time
Price rollback requires mandatory reason text and senior supervisor approval ΓÇö creates audit entry
Inventory gain/loss auto-calculated at every rate change: quantity on hand ├ù rate delta = revaluation entry
OGRA official price vs actual selling price variance tracker with daily reconciliation
Multi-product pricing: different rates for fleet customers, bulk purchasers, credit accounts
Domain 2: Stock & Supply Chain
  C    Stock & Inventory Management   [CRITICAL]
Goods Receipt Note (GRN) per delivery: supplier, date, time, vehicle registration, driver CNIC, tanker seal number, OGRA delivery note number
Each batch tagged permanently: purchase rate, quantity litres, tank loaded into, temperature (for volume correction), batch creation timestamp
Cost method: FIFO (default), LIFO, or weighted average ΓÇö configurable per station, affects P&L calculation
Dip-stick reading vs system quantity reconciliation: daily mandatory check, variance logged with reason
Evaporation allowance: configurable percentage per fuel type; system accounts for this in stock reconciliation
Spillage and sample deduction log: quantity, reason, authorised by ΓÇö affects stock balance
Tank-to-tank transfer: fully logged with source tank, destination tank, quantity, reason, approver
Minimum stock alert: configurable threshold per tank ΓåÆ auto-alert to station manager and owner
Physical stock audit module: blind count entry ΓåÆ system reveals expected quantity ΓåÆ discrepancy documented
Stock ageing: batches older than configurable days flagged for quality review
  D    Supplier Management   [CORE]
Supplier master: PSMC, PSO, APL, Shell, custom ΓÇö NTN, STRN, bank account, credit terms, contact hierarchy
Purchase orders with agreed rate, quantity, requested delivery window, authorised by
3-way matching: Purchase Order ΓåÆ Goods Receipt Note ΓåÆ Supplier Invoice (all 3 must match before payment approval)
Supplier payment ledger: amount, method (bank transfer/cash/cheque/RTGS), reference number, date, processed by
Outstanding payable tracker: amount due per supplier, due date, days overdue, penalty clause
Supplier rate history: every quoted price archived with date ΓÇö no rate can be disputed without evidence
Discount and rebate tracking: agreed rebate per litre ΓåÆ auto-applied to payment calculation
Supplier performance: delivery accuracy (quantity vs PO), on-time delivery rate, quality incidents
Domain 3: Operations & Sales
  E    Salesman & Shift Control   [CRITICAL]
Salesman master: full name, father's name, CNIC, photo, phone, emergency contact, bank account, station assignment, role
Authentication: biometric fingerprint + 6-digit PIN (dual factor), with FIDO2 hardware key support for managers
Shift open protocol: salesman logs in ΓåÆ system captures meter reading for every assigned nozzle ΓåÆ records cash in hand from previous shift ΓåÆ creates shift record
Shift close protocol: meter readings recaptured ΓåÆ cash counted by denomination ΓåÆ digital payment slips submitted ΓåÆ system calculates expected cash ΓåÆ flags any discrepancy immediately
Cash short/excess: any difference requires immediate reason entry, photo of cash count, and shift manager signature
Nozzle handover: meter reading signed by outgoing and incoming salesman ΓåÆ both signatures captured (digital or photo)
Salesman daily scorecard: litres sold, cash handled, discrepancies, customer complaints, shift punctuality
Leave and absent tracking: leave application ΓåÆ manager approval ΓåÆ automatic shift reassignment trigger
Performance history: every metric stored permanently for employment record and fraud detection
  F    Sales & Transaction Engine   [CRITICAL]
Every sale transaction records: timestamp (millisecond), salesman ID, shift ID, nozzle ID, fuel type, litres (4 decimal places), active rate ID, amount, payment method
Payment split per transaction: one sale can be partially cash + partially Easypaisa + partially credit ΓÇö all splits stored
Payment methods: Cash, Easypaisa, JazzCash, UPaisa, HBL Konnect, bank card (Visa/Mastercard/UnionPay), credit account
Vehicle registration capture: optional for retail, mandatory for fleet/credit customers
Receipt generation: QR-code receipt per transaction ΓÇö scannable by customer to verify authenticity
Loyalty customer programme: registered customer ID on transaction ΓåÆ running balance ΓåÆ monthly statement
Bulk / wholesale invoice: multi-line invoice with VAT/tax breakdown, approval workflow before issuance
Void/correction workflow: no silent voids ΓÇö requires reason, authorisation, and creates counter-entry
Offline mode: tablet/POS continues recording with local timestamp; syncs in order when connection restores; conflicts resolved by server timestamp

Domain 7: AI & Fraud Prevention
  M    AI Fraud & Anomaly Detection Engine   [AI / ML]
The AI engine maintains a rolling 90-day statistical baseline for every measurable parameter. Any reading beyond 2 standard deviations triggers automated investigation flags:
Salesman sales volume anomaly: detects if salesman sells 15%+ below their own statistical average
Meter reading manipulation: catches meter readings that go down, stay flat, or spike beyond physical pump flow rate
Cash short pattern: identifies salesmen with recurring 'round number' shortages (e.g. always exactly PKR 500 or PKR 1,000 short)
Price boundary fraud: detects sales recorded at old rate within the first or last minute of a rate change
Ghost transaction detection: sales with no corresponding nozzle meter movement
Supplier quantity fraud: delivered quantity vs invoiced quantity vs GRN vs tank dip-stick ΓÇö all four must agree
Digital payment ghost receipts: system shows digital payment received but merchant portal and bank show nothing
Nozzle calibration drift: litres dispensed per minute outside certified calibration range triggers calibration alert
Unusual hours detection: transactions at times when station schedule shows closed ΓÇö immediate alert
Week-on-week revenue break: >20% drop in any station's revenue without a documented reason triggers owner call
Natural language explanation engine: generates human-readable explanation of every anomaly ΓÇö e.g. 'Station 2 lost PKR 12,400 on Tuesday. Salesman Imran submitted PKR 8,000 less cash than meter shows. Additionally, 220 litres of HSD cannot be reconciled between GRN and meter sales.'
Predictive stock-out warning: 48-hour advance notice before any tank reaches minimum level, based on current consumption trend
Supplier price spike alert: new purchase price more than 3% above OGRA official rate triggers verification request

  SECTION 04  |  End-to-End Data Flow
  How Every Litre & Rupee Is Traced A to Z
4.1  Complete Traceability Chain
The following chains represent the complete, unbreakable flow of data through the system. Every link in each chain is a permanent database record.
Stock & Supply Chain:
[Supplier Invoice + Rate]  ΓåÆ  [GRN Created (batch ID + purchase rate)]  ΓåÆ  [Tank Stock Updated]  ΓåÆ  [Supplier Payable Created]  ΓåÆ  [Payment Matched]
Price Change Impact Chain:
[Owner Approves New Rate]  ΓåÆ  [Effective Time Recorded]  ΓåÆ  [Active Rate Table Updated All Nozzles]  ΓåÆ  [Stock Revaluation Entry Auto-Created]  ΓåÆ  [P&L Impact Logged]
Shift Sales Chain:
[Salesman Logs In (biometric)]  ΓåÆ  [Opening Meter Readings Captured]  ΓåÆ  [Sales Recorded (each: nozzle + rate + payment)]  ΓåÆ  [Shift Closed: Meter Difference Calculated]  ΓåÆ  [Cash Reconciliation: Expected vs Submitted]
Cash to Bank Chain:
[Cash/Digital Collected in Shift]  ΓåÆ  [Deposited to Safe/Bank (slip captured)]  ΓåÆ  [Bank Statement Imported]  ΓåÆ  [System Auto-Matches to Deposit Record]  ΓåÆ  [Unmatched Items Flagged for Investigation]
  Traceability Guarantee: Given any single sale transaction, the system can show ΓÇö in one click ΓÇö which salesman made it, on which nozzle, at what price (and why that price was active at that exact second), from which stock batch at what purchase cost, what the gross margin was on that litre, how the payment was collected, when it reached the bank, whether it has been reconciled, and whether any anomaly was flagged. This chain is permanent and unbreakable for the lifetime of the system.
